Our Story

Founded in 2015 by former graphic designer Ellie Vance, The Crooked Spoon Bakery began as a passion project in a small Oakhaven kitchen. Our commitment to handcrafted quality and local ingredients quickly earned us a devoted following.

Today, we continue to blend artisanal techniques with creative flair, bringing a touch of whimsy to every loaf, pastry, and cake we create.
